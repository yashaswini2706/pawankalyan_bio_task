About

Pawan Kalyan (born 2 September 1971) is an Indian actor, film producer, and politician known for his works in Telugu cinema. He is the younger brother of renowned actor Chiranjeevi. Pawan made his film debut in 1996 with *Akkada Ammayi Ikkada Abbayi*. He gained popularity with the film *Tholi Prema* (1998), which won the National Film Award. Apart from films, he is also known for his philanthropic activities and political journey. He founded the Jana Sena Party in 2014 and remains a key political figure in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

career

Pawan Kalyan started his acting journey in 1996 and rose to stardom quickly due to his unique style and impactful performances. He became a youth icon with films like *Thammudu*, *Badri*, and *Kushi*. His comeback film *Gabbar Singh* (2012) was a massive success and reinstated his popularity. He is also a trained martial artist and often choreographs action sequences in his films. Major Milestones in Film Career: First Major Role: His breakthrough role was in *Tholi Prema* (1998), which won a National Award and brought him immense recognition. Success in the 2000s: Pawan delivered several successful films like *Kushi*, *Jalsa*, and *Gabbar Singh*. His role in *Attarintiki Daredi* (2013) became one of the highest-grossing Telugu films at the time.
